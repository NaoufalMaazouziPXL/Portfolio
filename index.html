About me

A creative designer with passion

for creating visually compelling websites

Hello! My name is Naoufal, and I am passionate about web design and UX/UI design. With a strong background in this field, I possess experience and proficiency in various design tools such as Figma and Adobe XD. My skill set also includes working in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, allowing me to bring my designs to life with interactive and engaging features.

One of my greatest strengths is my keen eye for detail. I believe that even the smallest elements can significantly impact the overall user experience, and I strive to create visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces that captivate and delight users. I have a solid understand of visual hierarchy, components library, wire framing, visual designing, prototyping, high and low -fidelity and more!
